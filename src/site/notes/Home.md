Hello! 👋 You've found my digital grove—an evolving space where I cultivate ideas, document my research, and think in public.

This is a non-linear collection meant for wandering. Instead of a chronological feed, thoughts here are connected by meaning—allowing you to jump between concepts, follow trails of curiosity, and discover how different ideas intersect.

  > _"Not a feed, but a forest. Each note is a seed I’ve tended, each link a path to wander."_

## 🌱 How to Explore

Think of this space as a network of interconnected thoughts. You can wander freely, but here is a quick guide to help you navigate:

- **Follow the Links:** The best way to explore is to simply follow the internal links that interest you most. Let your curiosity guide you!
- **Check the Connections:** Look for connected concepts or use the search bar to find specific topics.
- **Embrace the Mess:** Some notes are just rough ideas or works-in-progress, while others are more fully formed.

## 🪴 Stages of Growth
  
Notes in this garden are always evolving. They exist in different stages of maturity:

- 🌱 **Seedlings:** Rough ideas, fleeting thoughts, and early drafts.

- 🌿 **Budding:** Developing ideas with some structure and connected concepts.

- 🌳 **Evergreen:** Well-developed, mature content that has stood the test of time.

I use this to connect the dots between what I’m learning and doing. Feel free to step in, explore the trails, and follow the threads that interest you most! 🚀🔍
